SURFACE TO AIR

Controls

  • Move Target: Mouse or Arrow Keys
  • Shoot: Spacebar or Click
  • Pause and Play: "P"
  • Reset: "R"
  • Mute / Unmute Background Music: "M" or Click Sound Button
Headphones Recommended
